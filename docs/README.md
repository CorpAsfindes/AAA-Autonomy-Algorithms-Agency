---

## Note on Language

Field evidence — including signed validation instruments, session photographs, and qualitative observations — is collected in Spanish.

This is an intentional methodological decision, not a limitation. The AAA system works directly with Spanish-speaking community leaders in Medellín's Comunas 12 and 13. Requiring participants to engage in English would create an accessibility barrier that contradicts the project's core values of territorial sovereignty and community agency.

The methodological documents in this folder are provided in English for international reviewers. The field instruments used with participants are functionally identical — translated into Spanish to ensure full comprehension and authentic engagement from community leaders.

This bilingual approach reflects a broader principle of the AAA project: **technology must adapt to communities, not the other way around.**
