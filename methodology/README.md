# Methodology — AAA Workshop Guides

This folder contains the intervention workshop guides for each dimension of the AAA Index. Each workshop is designed as a 90-minute Participatory Action Research (PAR) session, structured to address the specific risks detected by the AAA diagnostic system.

## Structure

| Folder | Dimension | Trigger |
|--------|-----------|---------|
| `autonomy/` | Digital Autonomy | Score below 8/20 on Autonomia_AAA |
| `algorithm/` | Algorithmic Awareness | Score below 8/20 on Algoritmo_AAA |
| `agency/` | Collective Agency | Score below 8/20 on Agencia_AAA |

## How to Use

1. Run the AAA diagnostic engine (`python src/main.py`) with your organization's data.
2. Identify which dimensions scored below the risk threshold (8/20).
3. Select the corresponding workshop guide from this folder.
4. Use the Field Validation Instrument (`/docs/Field_Validation_Instrument.docx`) alongside the workshop.
5. Upload collected evidence to the `/evidence` folder after each session.

## Workshop Structure

All three workshops follow the same 5-phase structure for methodological coherence:

| Phase | Activity | Duration |
|-------|----------|----------|
| 1 | Sovereignty Framing | 15 min |
| 2 | Results Presentation | 20 min |
| 3 | Validation and Qualitative Dialogue | 40 min |
| 4 | Capacity Transfer and Sovereignty Toolkit | 15 min |
| 5 | Closing, Evidence Signing and Commitment | 10 min |

## Design Principles

**1. Technology must adapt to communities, not the other way around.**
All recommended tools are open-source, free, and designed for non-technical users.

**2. Diagnosis without action is not sovereignty.**
Each workshop closes with a concrete toolkit — participants leave with a specific tool to address their dimension's weakness, not just awareness of the problem.

**3. Evidence is community property.**
All field evidence is collected with participant consent and stored in an open repository. Communities retain the right to access, review, and correct their own data.
